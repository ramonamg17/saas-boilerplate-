How it works

Three steps to fluency

No textbooks. No grammar drills. Just natural phrases in context, read aloud by a native voice.

1

Choose a topic

Type anything you want to practice — ordering coffee, job interviews, weekend plans, travel. If you can name it, we can generate it.

2

AI builds your session

GPT-4 writes a set of natural, contextual phrases. A native-quality voice reads them aloud at a comfortable learning pace.

3

Listen and absorb

Stream your session live as it generates, or download the MP3 and take it anywhere — your commute, workout, or quiet hour.
